Getting Youtube Transcripts

12 May 2019

Here's a neat trick I just found out about: You can request the transcript of a YouTube video that has captions by going to More > Transcript.

Reading the transcript is often a nice alternative to sitting through a video of someone talking, even in x2.

Unfortunately, it's still impossible to request automatic captionning for videos where the author didn't enable it. I wish they'd change that.
