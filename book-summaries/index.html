Book Summaries

24 Sep 2016

I like to read a lot, notably books about psychology, philosophy, and other actionable topics. However, I often feel that these book dilute their ideas too much, in order to make a "proper" (fat) book out of it.

But now, I have the cure. This list of book summary websites covers a large range of books in the topics that interest me.

Here's a copy of the list for posterity:

  • BookSummaryClub
  • Derek Sivers' Books Read
  • Farnam Street
  • 4 Minute Books
  • Actionable Books
  • Blinkist (paid)
  • Deconstructing Excellence (Wayback Machine)

The first four websites are great, but the selection is limited.

Actionable Books has a lot of things, but the summaries only include 3/4 bullet points and sometimes fail to capture the essence of the book, as far as I've seen.

Blinkist seemed like the most well-rounded option. I made a trial account and was quite satisfied with the two summaries I read.
